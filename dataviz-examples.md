# Data visualization examples

## Government Debt-to-GDP Ratios

The below visualization depicts trends in the debt-to-GDP ratio for several nations. Viewers will note the spike in ratios across countries during 2020 and 2021 (i.e., the onset and height of the COVID pandemic), suggesting a need for further investigation of a potential relationship between COVID and national debt.
